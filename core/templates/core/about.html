About Nexus Connect

Nexus Connect is a digital innovation hub dedicated to empowering African technologists, entrepreneurs and researchers. We provide training, incubation support, hands-on labs and collaborative community events focused on Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Software Engineering and Embedded Systems.

Our Mission

To build and scale a thriving technology ecosystem in Africa through education, R&D, startup support, and community-led innovation.

What We Offer

  • Practical workshops and bootcamps
  • Prototyping labs for robotics & embedded systems
  • AI model prototyping & deployment support
  • Startup incubation & mentorship
  • Community events, hackathons and partnerships
